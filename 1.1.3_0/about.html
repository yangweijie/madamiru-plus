🎯 About This Extension

NexVid is a browser extension that enhances video quality in real-time using advanced upscaling algorithms and visual effects. It applies GPU-accelerated filters and shaders to improve the viewing experience of anime, movies, and other video content directly in your browser.

✨ Key Features

  • Real-time Upscaling: Multiple quality profiles from ultra-fast to maximum quality
  • Visual Filters: Brightness, saturation, contrast, grayscale, sepia, hue rotation, and blur
  • Creative Shaders: 20+ retro effects including CRT, VHS, glitch, chromatic aberration, and more
  • Preset Management: Save and load your favorite configurations
  • Multi-language: English, French, Spanish, and Chinese support
  • Performance Control: Adjustable FPS targeting for optimal performance

🎮 Shader Effects

20+ GPU-accelerated visual effects organized by category:

📺 CRT / Retro

Scanlines, curvature effects

📼 VHS / Analog

Glitches, tracking errors, noise, color bleeding

🌈 Chromatic Effects

RGB splitting, chromatic aberration, color shifts

✨ Glow / Bloom

Soft glows, light effects

🎞️ Film / Grain

Film grain, digital noise

💥 Glitch / Distortion

Datamosh, scanline distortion, mirror effects

🎨 Artistic

Pixelation, kaleidoscope, old film projector
